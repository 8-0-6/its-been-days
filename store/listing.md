## Detailed description (max 16,000 chars)

You have 47 tabs open. You know it. Some of them have been there for weeks.

It's Been Days shows you exactly how long each tab has been inactive — and makes it trivially easy to close the ones you've been ignoring. Closed something by accident? It's in your history. Find it and reopen it in two keystrokes.

**What it does:**

• Shows your open tabs ranked by inactivity — the oldest ones at the top
• Tracks days since you last visited each tab
• Highlights tabs you haven't touched in over 10 days (threshold is adjustable)
• Archives every tab you close, so nothing is ever truly gone
• Lets you search open and closed tabs from anywhere with ⌘ Shift Space

**The overlay:**
Press ⌘ Shift Space (Ctrl+Shift+Space on Windows) on any page to open a floating search panel. Type to filter your open and closed tabs. Arrow keys to navigate, Enter to switch or reopen, Delete to remove an archive entry. No mouse required.

**The cleanup view:**
Open the overlay and you will see stale tabs, open tabs, and recently archived tabs in one place. "Suggested" tabs are the ones worth closing. Click × to close and archive, or "Close N" to sweep stale tabs. Made a mistake? You have 5 seconds to undo.

**Privacy:**
Everything stays on your device. No tab data is ever sent to any server. No account required. Optional history import is local-only and explicit opt-in.

**Pricing:**
Free forever. No paywall. No trial lock. No subscription. If you want to support development, there is an optional Buy Me a Coffee link in settings.
